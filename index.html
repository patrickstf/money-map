FAQ

Common questions.

Straight answers, no fluff.

Is FyndrWealth really free?
Yes — completely free, forever. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no trial period that converts to a subscription. The free plan includes every core feature. Pro and Family plans are coming soon for users who want advanced features.
Do you need my bank login or account number?
No. FyndrWealth never connects to your bank. You enter your numbers manually, which many people prefer — it keeps your banking credentials completely private and gives you full control over what data is tracked.
Where is my data stored?
If you create a free account, your data is stored securely on Supabase — a trusted cloud database used by thousands of applications. Guest mode data is stored only in your browser and disappears when you close the tab.
How does the AI advisor work?
The AI advisor is powered by Claude (by Anthropic). Every time you ask a question, it reads your actual financial data — income, expenses, debts, goals — and gives you personalized advice based on your real numbers. Not generic tips.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. FyndrWealth is mobile-first and works on any browser on any device. You can also add it to your iPhone or Android home screen for an app-like experience. A native iOS and Android app is in development.
Can I use it without creating an account?
Yes — guest mode lets you use every feature immediately with no signup. The only difference is your data won't be saved if you close the browser. Creating a free account takes 30 seconds and saves everything to the cloud.
